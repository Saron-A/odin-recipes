Lasagna Al Forno

Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish that has gained worldwide popularity as the ultimate comfort food. Though it requires time and effort to prepare, the rich, layered flavors make it well worth it. This hearty pasta casserole consists of alternating layers of tender lasagna sheets, flavorful sauces, and a variety of delicious fillings. The classic lasagna al forno is made with four key components: Pasta, Sauce, Cheese and Bechamel. These layers are carefully assembled and repeated until the dish is full, then baked to perfection until the top turns golden brown and irresistibly bubbly. The result is a warm, comforting meal with a delicious contrast of textures—soft pasta, creamy cheese, and rich, savory sauce. Whether served at family dinners, festive gatherings, or special occasions, lasagna never fails to bring people together with its satisfying flavors.

Ingredients

  • Meat
    Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and Garlic
    An onion and two cloves of garlic cooked with the meat
  • Tomato products
    A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar
    Two table spoons of white sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
    Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
    Store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
    Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese
  • Egg
    One egg to help bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out when cut into it

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Mix the ricotta cheese with an egg and some parsley
  4. Layer the lasagna accordingly
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta cheese mixture
    4. Mozzarella cheese
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
